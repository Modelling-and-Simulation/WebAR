AR MODEL LOADING COMPARITION


The goal of this collection is to compare the loading times of the 3D models(using localhost) and hosted platform(https://arloader.000webhostapp.com/).

Viewing these AR examples will require a device with a camera and one or both of the Hiro and Kanji marker patterns (either printed or displayed on a second device). Also this collection consist of options in image tracking with NFT, loading content quality wise and change the augmenting content
